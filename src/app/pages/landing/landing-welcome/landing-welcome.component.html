Welcome To Southern Ridge

You may not stay for a long time, but you'll be sure to have a good time! We're a family and pet friendly park with over 1,000 acres of property to ride, from hills, to dirt roads, to swamp, we have a little bit of everything for every rider. When the sun is shinin' you can cool off in the river or when it starts to chill out there's a bonfire pit to warm you up, and maybe show off your dance moves. We have RV sites (reservation required) as well as primitive camping and bath houses so you can stay and enjoy yourself, so come on and get your mud on!
